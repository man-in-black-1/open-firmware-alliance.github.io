What is Open Firmware Alliance?


Who We Are


Open Firmware Alliance is a service for erase old proprietary firmware and operating system with probably backdoor and not verified code, with a open source code, is possible verify the code, in case you are a programmer.

We are part of the collective movement cyberpunk

For more about this movement click here 

What We Sell


We sell laptops with firmware open source without backdoor, possibility configuration a custom operating system and other configuration

Disk Encryption


We offer an optional service for mitigate the Man in the Middle attack during the shipping of your device, we encrypt the memory ssd with your custom password, possibility send the sensitive data with Pgp or Gpg encryption.
