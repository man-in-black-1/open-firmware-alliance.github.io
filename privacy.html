Privacy Policy for Open Firmware Alliance

Effective Date: August 2025

At Open Firmware Alliance, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our online store and purchase products using Bitcoin (BTC). By using our services, you agree to the terms of this policy.

Information we collect

Personal information:
We do not collect personal information such as your name or phone number when you make a purchase using Bitcoin.

Transaction information:
We collect information about your transactions, including the amount of Bitcoin used and details of the products purchased.
We do not use cookies or tracking technologies:  Our site does not use this technology. 

How we use your information
Transaction processing: We use your transaction information to process your orders and deliver products.
Customer support: If you contact us for assistance, we may use the information you provide to help you.

No data recording policy
We do not keep any records of your transactions or personal information. All transactions made using Bitcoin are processed without storing any identifiable information.

At Open Firmware Alliance, we prioritize the privacy and security of our clients' data. To ensure that your information is handled responsibly, we have implemented a policy where all client data is automatically deleted from our servers after 30 days. This practice not only helps us maintain a secure environment but also aligns with our commitment to safeguarding your privacy. You can shop with confidence, knowing that your information will not be retained longer than necessary.
